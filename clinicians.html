For Clinicians

LiquidMammo is a microRNA‑based liquid biopsy designed to complement imaging. The reporting provides a malignancy probability estimate and signature‑pattern insights to help guide next steps (biopsy/imaging remain confirmatory).

Clinical context

  • False‑negatives occur in mammography (≈1 in 8 overall) and are more likely in dense breasts.
  • False‑negative rates have increased in large U.S. datasets over recent years.
  • Detection yield with mammography is typically 2–7 cancers per 1,000 screens, with lower yield in younger/dense‑breasted cohorts.
  • False‑positive burden over a decade is substantial (≥1 false positive for ~50–60% of screened women).
