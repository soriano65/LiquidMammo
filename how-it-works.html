How LiquidMammo Works

  1. Order — A clinician orders LiquidMammo during routine care.
  2. Blood Draw — Standard venipuncture; no compression or ionizing radiation.
  3. microRNA Panel — Plasma is assayed for a proprietary microRNA signature associated with breast cancer biology.
  4. AI‑assisted Analysis — Model estimates probability of malignancy and provides signature insights (“tipification”) to help guide next steps.
  5. Clinician Report — Clear summary to complement clinical judgment and imaging; biopsy/imaging remain confirmatory.

Why an adjunct test?

Screening mammograms can miss cancers (false‑negatives) and the risk is higher in dense breasts. Large U.S. datasets suggest false‑negative rates have increased over time, underscoring the need for complementary approaches.

This is especially relevant for younger women: roughly 50–74% of women aged 40–49 have dense breasts, and mammography sensitivity in extremely dense tissue falls to about 61%. Among women under 56, half of interval cancers are attributed to density‑related masking (NEJM 2007). Full evidence →
