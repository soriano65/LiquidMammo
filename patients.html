A kinder, simpler way to screen—alongside your doctor

No compression. No radiation. LiquidMammo is a routine blood draw ordered by your clinician to complement imaging pathways.

Why this matters

  • False‑negative mammograms occur and are more likely in dense breasts.
  • False‑positive callbacks are common over years of screening.
  • Dense breasts are associated with higher interval cancers (found between screenings).
  • Most women under 50 have dense breasts—about 50–74% of women in their 40s—which can lower mammography accuracy and increase the chance of a missed cancer (CDC; BCSC).

Frequently asked

Does this replace mammograms? No. LiquidMammo is designed to complement, not replace, imaging and clinical judgment.

Is it available today? We’re advancing validation for CLIA LDT availability. Your clinician will advise when and how to use it appropriately.
