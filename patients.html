Information for Patients

LiquidMammo™ is being developed as a simple blood test that works alongside mammography to strengthen early breast‑cancer detection. It is non‑invasive, does not require breast compression, and can be added to a routine blood draw at your doctor’s office.

LiquidMammo is under development as a CLIA Laboratory Developed Test (LDT) and is not yet FDA‑cleared/approved.

Why consider a supplemental test?

Mammography saves lives, but certain factors can reduce its sensitivity. Dense breast tissue, younger age, and individual biological differences can make cancers harder to detect on imaging. A molecular signal from the bloodstream—particularly microRNA changes—may offer additional information for you and your clinician.

Non‑invasive

A standard blood draw—no compression, no radiation, no preparation.

Complements mammography

Provides a biological signal where imaging may be limited, especially in dense breasts.

Clinician‑ordered

Integrated into clinical care—designed to support, not replace, standard screening guidelines.

How the test works

1 — Simple blood draw

Your clinician orders the test during a routine appointment. A blood sample is collected like any standard lab test.

2 — microRNA analysis

The sample is analyzed for microRNA patterns associated with breast‑cancer biology, including markers that may shift before imaging findings.

3 — Clinician‑interpreted results

Your clinician receives a report with the result and guidance for next steps, such as routine follow‑up or additional imaging if indicated.

Who might benefit?

  • People with dense breasts (common under age 50).
  • Those who want a non‑invasive supplemental test alongside mammography.
  • Individuals with family history or elevated risk who want more frequent monitoring.
  • People who find mammograms painful or avoid screening due to discomfort.
  • Women under 40 who are not yet recommended for routine mammograms but have risk factors.

Always discuss with your clinician whether a supplemental test is appropriate for your specific risk profile.

Safety & privacy

Your data

Only your clinician can order the test. Results are shared with your care team and handled under strict privacy and data‑security requirements.

Sample handling

All samples are processed in certified laboratories under controlled conditions with strict chain‑of‑custody and quality protocols.

No radiation

LiquidMammo requires no imaging, compression, or radiation—just a routine blood draw.

How to access the test

Enrollment is currently limited as we complete our validation studies. You can sign up for updates or participate in early access through your clinician.
