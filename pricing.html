Pricing & Access

LiquidMammo™ is currently in validation and early‑access rollout. During this period, we offer a simplified and transparent pricing model for both patients and clinicians. Final pricing may evolve as clinical performance is established and reimbursement pathways expand.

LiquidMammo is being developed as a CLIA Laboratory Developed Test (LDT). It is not yet FDA‑cleared/approved.

Pricing structure

Self‑pay (Early Access)

$295

Early‑access pricing for patients whose clinicians participate in the validation program. Includes blood draw processing, microRNA analysis, and clinician‑ready report.

Clinician partner program

$0–$195

Discounted rates for practices enrolling in validation collaborations (limited availability). Includes training kits, clinical support materials, and dedicated onboarding.

Future insurance coverage

Insurance and reimbursement pathways are in development. Coverage will depend on clinical validation, payer policy, and medical‑necessity documentation.

What your price includes

microRNA profiling

High‑sensitivity multi‑marker molecular analysis performed in a CLIA lab.

Sample processing

Plasma stabilization, quality control, and extraction included.

Clinical report

A structured clinician‑ready interpretation with guidance for follow‑up.

Support

Optional onboarding for clinicians; direct helpdesk for early‑access patients.

Who should consider the test?

  • Patients with dense breasts (BI‑RADS C/D).
  • Patients seeking a non‑invasive supplemental tool to increase screening confidence.
  • Patients with family history or elevated risk profiles.
  • Women under 40 with risk indicators but not yet eligible for routine mammograms.
  • Clinics serving under‑screened populations or patients avoiding mammography discomfort.
