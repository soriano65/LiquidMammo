Privacy & Compliance

MultiOmics Inc. maintains strict privacy, consent, and security practices to protect patient and clinical data associated with LiquidMammo™, our microRNA‑based supplemental screening test. The information below outlines our compliance approach, data‑handling safeguards, and participant protections.

LiquidMammo is being developed as a CLIA Laboratory Developed Test (LDT). It is not yet FDA‑cleared or FDA‑approved.

HIPAA alignment

  • All Protected Health Information (PHI) is handled under HIPAA‑aligned workflows.
  • Data are encrypted in transit and at rest.
  • Only authorized clinical and laboratory personnel have access to PHI.
  • Audit logs track access, modification, and usage of sensitive information.
  • No PHI is shared externally without proper authorization and purpose limitation.

Consent & data use

All patient samples and associated information used in the development, validation, or operation of LiquidMammo follow informed‑consent pathways that define permitted uses and data‑sharing restrictions.

  • Consent scopes are respected without exception.
  • Secondary use requires alignment with original consent language.
  • De‑identified data may be used for internal validation and quality assurance.
  • External collaborators receive only de‑identified datasets.

Data protection safeguards

  • Industry‑standard encryption and access controls.
  • Strict SOPs for laboratory and data workflows.
  • Segregated environments for PHI and de‑identified research data.
  • Routine internal and external security reviews.
  • Secure deletion and retention policies based on regulatory guidance.

CLIA LDT compliance

LiquidMammo is being developed and validated under CLIA Laboratory Developed Test (LDT) practices. Compliance measures include:

  • CLIA‑certified laboratory environment.
  • Validation of analytical accuracy, precision, and reproducibility.
  • Ongoing proficiency testing and process documentation.
  • Quality management and continuous improvement programs.

Data access requests

Researchers or collaborators seeking dataset access may submit a brief concept note to begin review. Only de‑identified datasets are eligible for release.

Frequently asked

Do you sell or share patient data?

No. Data are not sold or shared for commercial purposes.

Do research collaborators receive PHI?

No. Only de‑identified datasets may be shared after review and approval.

How long is data retained?

Retention follows CLIA, HIPAA, and state regulatory requirements.
