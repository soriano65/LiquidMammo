Privacy & Compliance

Regulatory status

LiquidMammo is being developed as a CLIA Laboratory Developed Test (LDT). It is not yet FDA‑cleared or FDA‑approved; use is at the ordering clinician’s discretion within applicable regulations.

Data protection

We follow HIPAA‑aligned processes. Research datasets used for model development are de‑identified and protected by access controls and audit trails.
